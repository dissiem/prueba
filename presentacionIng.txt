virtual reality is a computer enviroment generated with scenes and objets that appear to be real,
making the user feel they are inmersed in their surroundings.
this enviroment is perceived through a device known as a Virtual reality headset o helmet


How it works:
The user provides input to the system by making movementes in physical 3d space or manipulating tools,
sensors, or devices in 3d space without regard for what this input is used to do or control
